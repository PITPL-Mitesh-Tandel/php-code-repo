We connect people to space & time by light

The work of our team deals with the issue of social place making. The environmental problems that we face have changed dramatically in the recent past, yet mankind has not. It is the timeless qualities of humanness and nature that provide continuity and familiarity among different cultures and different moments in history. 

It is our attempt through design to focus on these fundamental characteristics and needs to translate them into designed triggers that are appropriate to their context and cultural milieu. To understand the relation between our client and space users emotions and aspirations and the evocative aspect of built form, object or space, is the basis of Atelier dada's lighting design philosophy.


Value @ day & night

We crave for the best design experience, and we bring it forward through our artistic flair, best education, travels, observations, work experiences and inquiry by design. The fact that we are a young team willing to experiment and not restricting our field of services has enriched Atelier dada's portfolio with varied scale design problems. Atelier dada uses latest technologies and trends of collaborations for our clients specific design requirement, and find satisfactorily solutions by artists, architects, engineers, thinkers and designers. Our approach is to make applied research useful to provide designs with appropriate technology, sustainable, pragmatic and humanistic designs. 

Holistic lighting design solutions are achieved by understanding client’s aspirations, a vision for future, a relational concept for a unique sensorial and emotional interactive visual experience, technological resolution by experimentation and industry collaborations and ultimately a satisfactory service delivery with precise installation, programming and documentation for easy maintenance.
