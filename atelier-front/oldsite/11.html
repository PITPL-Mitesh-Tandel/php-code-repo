. "Dalal villas" private residences in Ahmedabad - in progress 2017

Atelier dada created decorative urban light furniture (pole, bollards, gate light, parking light) from pure wood, inspired by the architectural language of the villas designed by Apurva Amin Architects. Rest of the lighting elements is subtly hidden inside the landscape elements designed by Kunal Maniar & Associates.


. "Shilp Aaaron" commercial complex in Ahmedabad - in progress 2017

In a very cost effective yet energy saving solution, Atelier dada create a poetic scenography for Shilp Aaron commercial complex by integrating Electric fireflies inside the technical shafts. These simple custom fittings create a halo around the commercial complex, making it glow and distinguish in the immediate surrounding.

The lighting solution initially dynamic became static finally because of budget constraints. Main porches and landscape lighting still in progress.

. "SUMEL 6" Mega commercial complex  - Completed in 2015.

Site is located on the grounds of old mill compound in eastern part of Ahmedabad city. The facility comprises of nearly 1500 small and large retail outlets with G+4 floor high 10 long building blocks forming great perspectives to explore. The area around is challenging, dotted by many unorganized small shops and in a bit dilapidated form. Hence, it was very important to attract the buyers and make owners and tenants proud of their business address. The age old trees dotted around the edge between plot and the main roads were very impressive and potential source of exploration for us. The project includes also exclusive luminaire design for the project inspired from the genius loci. Info about "Aura" lighting concept will be communicated in our new website soon.
