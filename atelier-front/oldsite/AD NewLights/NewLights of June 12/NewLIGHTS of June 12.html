Lumière de Pierre - Light Art

"Lumiere de pierre", or "Light of stones" in french, is an artistic project in collaboration with our polyvalent artist Walid Zouari and the brilliant photographer Abderrazk Khechine. The project aims to challenge usual borders of perception at Bardo museum sculptures' gallery. Further, with other members of atelierdada, the 2Dimensional artworks of Walid and Abderrazek using the mixed media like painting, photograph and digital touchups would be made available to experience in a 3Dimension gallery space using special museographic light effects.
